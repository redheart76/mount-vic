Mt Vic Mountain Biking Trails

Mountain Biking a stones throw away from the CBD. Great to hit the trails at lunch time, or if the afternoon necessitates it.

Mt Vic is shared with walkers but there is plenty of room for everyone.Land managed by Wellington City Council.

Most is part of the town belt reserve.

Mount Victoria Wellington Lookout walk

The Mount Victoria walk takes you up Mount Victoria at 196 m for 360-degree scenic views of Wellington in New Zealand.

There are several routes you can take up Mount Victoria. They will lead you through bush or a pine forest before reaching the peak.
