Cable Car

The Wellington Cable Car is a funicular railway in Wellington, New Zealand, between Lambton Quay,

the main shopping street, and Kelburn, a suburb in the hills overlooking the central city, rising 120 m over a length of 612 m. The one way trip takes approximately five minutes.
